**Request to Postpone Trial:**

*   **Approved:** The request to postpone the trial is approved, and the court orders the trial is postponed as indicated in (3).
*   **Denied Reason:** The request to postpone the trial is denied because:

    *   The request was not filed at least 10 days before the hearing and did not include a good reason for the late request.
