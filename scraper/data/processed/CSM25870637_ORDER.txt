ORDER AFTER HEARING

THE CLAIM

This matter, filed on November 5, 2025, was heard on December 15, 2025 in Department 506 of the above-entitled court. The Plaintiff, Michelle Varnado, asks for damages in the amount of $12,500.00 arising from construction work performed by Defendant Stewart and his company, Bradford Construction, incident to the remodeling of the home of Defendants Shalek and Luby at 219 Holly Park Circle which is adjacent to the Plaintiff's property at 215 Holly Park Circle in the City of San Francisco.

Ms. Varnado, Mr Gerber and Ms. Luby each appeared in person and testified under oath. The Court has carefully considered the testimony of each party and makes findings as follows:

FINDINGS

1. Neither Defendant Stewart nor his business, Bradford Construction, is liable to Ms. Varnado for damages.

2. The remaining parties agreed in open court to resolve the dispute: Defendants Luby and her spouse, Nick Shalek, will pay Plaintiff the sum of $5,000.00 in return for a release of all claims and a dismissal of this matter with prejudice.

ORDER

The action shall be and the same hereby is CONTINUED to Friday, January 26, 2026 at 3:30 pm in Department 506 of this Court pending dismissal pursuant to settlement.
