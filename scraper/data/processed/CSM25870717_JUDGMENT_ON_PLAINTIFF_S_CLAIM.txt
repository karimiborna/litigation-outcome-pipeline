**SHORT TITLE:** Alissa Wang v. Julie Bautista

**CASE NUMBER:** CSM-25-870717

**ATTACHMENT (Number):** [This Attachment may be used with any Judicial Council form.]

1. Plaintiff Alissa Wang is entitled to her deposit back from master tenant in the amount of 1,325.00 from landlord Julie Bautista because she failed to offer a pre - move out inspection and failed to send an itemization of damages within 21 days of the date Alissa Wang moved out. Civil Code 1950.5.

2. Plaintiff Alissa Wang is entitled to interest on her deposit of 143.55. S.F. Administrative Code 49.2.

3. Plaintiff Alissa Wang is entitled to double damages under Civil Code 1950.51(1) for the landlord defendants bad faith conduct, including deprivation of services by locking up the office, retaliatory eviction notice, demand to sign new lease by 9/24 taking away the onsite office use, and threatening the tenant with reports to the police, resulting in damages of twice the deposit in the sum of 2,650.

4. The Court finds further damages requested to be ordinary moving expenses and court costs which are included in the Judgement.
