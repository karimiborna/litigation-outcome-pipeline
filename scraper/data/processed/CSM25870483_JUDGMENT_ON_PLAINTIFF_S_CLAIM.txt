**Alexander Goodkind**
**v.**
**Lan Le**

This matter was heard on November 13, 2025 in Department 506 of the above entitled court. Plaintiff Alexander Goodkind and Defendant Lan Le each appeared in person. Both testified under oath.

Defendant Le requested that the matter be dismissed against her, as she is not the Owner of the premises in question, but the Owner's agent. Her request is denied.

Plaintiff is awarded damages in the amount of $1,335.04 wrongfully withheld from his security deposit, plus costs totaling $125.00.

Damage amounts are: $184.04 (one day's rent); $550.00 (cost of hauling out furniture and other items Plaintiff had reason to believe he could leave when he moved out); and $600.00 (cleaning fees).
