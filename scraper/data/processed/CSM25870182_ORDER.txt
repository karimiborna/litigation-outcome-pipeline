On November 13, 2025, Respondent Grace Balkema appeared at the 11:00 a.m. ex parte calendar requesting a continuance of the hearing date for a small claims appeal from November 19, 2025, to December 3, 2025.

Respondent gave notice to Appellant Cameron Kambiz Razavi on November 6, 2025, via telephone. The Court finds Appellant was given adequate notice pursuant to California Rules of Court, Rules 3.1200 et seq., and did not appear at the hearing to object to the continuance.

For good cause appearing in Respondent’s papers, the application to continue is GRANTED.

The hearing date of November 19, 2025, is vacated and trial is continued to Wednesday December 3, 2025, at 9:00 a.m. in Room 525.
